About Holiday Getaway

Your trusted partner for exceptional hospitality experiences

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Holiday Getaway began as a vision to create extraordinary hospitality experiences across Sri Lanka's most beautiful destinations.

What started as a single boutique hotel in Colombo has grown into a premier hospitality chain with six stunning locations, each offering unique experiences while maintaining our commitment to exceptional service and luxury.

Today, we continue to set new standards in hospitality, combining modern amenities with authentic Sri Lankan warmth and culture.
